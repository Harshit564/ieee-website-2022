Electroviz

"No resistance can drop our potential”

IEEE PEC Student Branch presents “Electroviz”, the event under the flagship 3-day long “TechaDroit’22”.

The contest aims to test your knowledge on the fundamentals of electronics and electrical. Round 1 comprises of a general quiz followed by a shortlisting of the top 7 teams for a round table head-on buzzer.

Round 1: 11:30 AM -12:30 PM, 3rd April 2022
Venue: New Academic Block, PEC Chandigarh
Team Size: 1-3
Prizes: Top 2 teams will receive Prizes Worth 3k
Mode: OFFLINE
Results of Round 2 and timings shall be announced post Round 1.

All registered participants must join the telegram group: https://t.me/+D8dwyCApXwthMGVl

Come have fun, Test your knowledge & Buzz a bit!
