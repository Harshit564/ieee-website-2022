About Us

IEEE PEC is the student chapter of IEEE, world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity in Punjab Engineering College.. IEEE India Council is the umbrella organization, which takes care of activities of 55k+ members across various demographics of India.

The IEEE PEC Student Branch was started in 2001 and has since raised its bar by benefitting tech enthusiasts at its campus each day to achieve greater heights.The Student Branch has not only hosted events in the boundaries of its campus but has gone far beyond and has held events like the Chandigarh Sub-Section Congress, Region-10 Sponsored events and many more. It is one of the top notch societies at the PEC Campus and serves as a platform for undergrads, postgrads and faculty alike to enhance their knowledge in varied technical and non-technical domains.
